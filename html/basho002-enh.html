Basho Matsuo

蚤 のみ 虱 しらみ

馬 うま の 尿 しと する

枕 まくら もと

I arise early to lice

and fleas, when a horse pisses

Next to my pillow

Remarks

The original is more compressed than the translation: that the poet is awakened by the horse is only implicit.
